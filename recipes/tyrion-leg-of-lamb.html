Tyrion's Leg of Lamb

A juicy and cooked lamb leg, inspired by HBO's Game of Thrones

What better way to watch Game of Thrones than by devouring a recipe inspired by everyone's favorite imp. Just be careful not to call him that to his face or he'll set a dragon upon you.

Ingredients

  • 5 lb Leg of lamb
  • 1/4 cup Basil
  • 4 tbsp Garlic
  • 1/4 cup Golden raisins
  • 16 oz Spinach
  • 1 Salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Pine nuts
  • 1/3 cup Goat cheese

Steps

  1. Remove the leg bone from the leg of lamb.
  2. Chop up your spinach.
  3. Toss your chopped spinach into a pan heated over medium heat, along with the olive oil.
  4. Add your minced garlic to the pan and sauté it until it halves in volume, which should take approximately 2 minutes.
  5. Mix your sautéed spinach and garlic from the pan into a small bowl.
  6. Add the pine nuts, golden raisins, and goat cheese to the bowl as well.
  7. Combine the ingredients well.
  8. Next, take the mixture of sautéed spinach, garlic, pine nuts, golden raisins, and goat cheese, and generously smear it on the inside of the butterflied leg of lamb.
  9. Roll up some foil and wrap it around your meat, just as you would with cooking twine.
  10. Place your prepared lamb into a roasting pan and transfer it to the preheated oven set to 350°F.
  11. Allow the lamb to roast for approximately one to one and a half hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F.
  12. Once the internal temperature of your lamb’s leg reaches 145°F, it’s time to remove it from the oven. Let the leg rest for a minimum of 10 minutes before proceeding.
  13. After the rest, it’s time to slice the lamb, revealing its perfectly cooked and succulent interior.
  14. Serve the slices with pride, savoring the culmination of your culinary endeavors.
